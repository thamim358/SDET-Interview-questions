🖨️ How to Convert HTML Files to Premium PDFs

These files are designed with professional dark-theme styling that prints beautifully as PDFs. Follow these steps:

• Step 1: Open the HTML file in Google Chrome (right-click → Open with → Chrome)
• Step 2: Press Ctrl+P (Windows) or Cmd+P (Mac) to open the Print dialog
• Step 3: In the Print dialog, change the destination to "Save as PDF"
• Step 4: Under "More settings", set:
  • Paper size: A4
  • Margins: None
  • Scale: Default
  • Check "Background graphics" to preserve the dark theme
• Step 5: Click Save — your premium PDF is ready!
💡 Tip: The files are optimized for print — dark theme, question cards with page-break-inside: avoid, and print-specific styles. The PDFs will look like premium FAANG training material.
