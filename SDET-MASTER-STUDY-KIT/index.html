Master all P1 (red) questions first — these appear in nearly every interview. Then P2 (amber) for breadth. Review P3 (blue) for senior roles.

JavaScript → TypeScript → Playwright → Framework Design. Then branch to SQL, DevOps, System Design, and AI Testing depending on your target role.

💡 Certification Strategy for 4-Years Exp SDET

Don't collect certs — collect capabilities. Prioritize 1 Cloud (AWS/Azure) + 1 DevOps (CKAD/GitHub Actions) + 1 Testing (ISTQB CT-AI). That combination proves you can deploy, orchestrate, and validate modern software systems. Free certs (Playwright 101) are great for resume keywords; paid certs (AWS DevOps Pro, CKAD) move the salary needle.
